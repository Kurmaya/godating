About Us

In today's dating scene, we're all stuck in a never-ending cycle of swiping through profiles that look more
and more alike.  

The stats don't lie: Roughly 20 million Indians used dating apps just five years ago. This figure surged by a
massive 293% since then and hit 82.4 million in 2023. However, a significant number of users are
dissatisfied with their current dating app experience.

Why the disconnect? Traditional dating apps leave us feeling like cogs in a machine, endlessly swiping with
no real connection or control. Who even remembers the last profile that sparked a genuine interest?

With Go Dating, we're ditching the endless texting for a more human approach – a virtual speed dating
experience where you connect with interesting people and book confirmed dates. Imagine the excitement
of knowing your date is actually showing up, ready for a fun, real-life interaction!

Plus, with Go Dating, you're in control. Set your preferences (Host or Guest), choose who initiates dates,
and spark genuine connections based on shared interests. 

No more ghosting, no more awkward first-message struggles. Just real people, real dates, and a chance to
rewrite your love story.
