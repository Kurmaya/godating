Is Go Dating safe?

We prioritize your safety. Go Dating will utilize Aadhar verification (coming soon) to confirm user identities. This adds a layer of trust and security to your dating experience. br Additionally, we offer in-app chat functionality and reporting tools that allow you to flag inappropriate behavior and ensure a positive environment.

Do I have to pay to use Go Dating?

Go Dating is a free-to-use mobile application! You can create a profile, browse matches, and chat with people you're interested in at no cost. 
However, there's a unique twist: There is a fee to book and schedule your date at a partner venue. 
  Here's how it works:
→ Host: The person who initiates the date becomes the host. They book the date by paying a fee. This fee covers the platform's maintenance and ensures a genuine interest in meeting up.
→ Guest: The person who receives the date invitation and confirms their attendance becomes the Guest. They are not charged a fee.

How can I sign up for Go Dating?

Go Dating is launching soon! Follow us for updates on our social media platforms (add links). Once the app launches, download it from the App Store or Google Play Store. Create your profile, start browsing matches, and get ready to find your perfect someone!

What makes Go Dating different from other apps?

Go Dating focuses on confirmed dates, eliminating the frustration of endless swiping and online chat. You can lock in a date and time with someone who's genuinely interested in meeting you in person, increasing your chances of a successful connection.

Who is a Host?

A person who always takes the first step of initiation to meet the Guest and know about them.
A Host express their interest by paying a certain value of money to meet the Guest IRL as per the Guests availability.
The Guest can redeem 80% of that value in the Go Shopping section (In-app marketplace) to avail their favorite gifts OR Can utilize the money for the Bill of the Date.

Who is a Guest?

A person who compares their time value and money with value proposition.
A Guest on Go Dating decides the Price, Availability & Spots to meet the person they would like to go out with.
A Guest on Go Dating brings value to a date by consulting or Advising the Host on Dating, Lifestyle, Fitness, Networking services, etc.
A Guest on Go Dating decides how to utilize the money of the Host by either chosing to pay the bill at a restraunt (Free Date) or keeping it as a Gift. (In Both the cases go dating takes 20% of the total amount.)
A Guest on Go Dating aspires to achieve their short term and long term goals by utilizing the Hosts money in the Go Shopping Section to shop Home and lifestyle assets.

What is the review and rating feature all about?

The review and rating feature plays a crucial role in maintaining a positive and genuine user experience on Go Dating. After a completion of the date, both the Host and Guest will have the opportunity to anonymously rate and review their experience. This helps:
→ Improve Matches: Reviews can highlight positive qualities or areas for improvement, allowing users to make informed decisions about potential dates.
→ Maintain a High Standard: Knowing their profiles will be reviewed motivates users to be respectful and genuine during their interactions.
→ Build Trust: Positive reviews from past dates can increase someone's credibility and chances of securing more matches.

How to cancel a date after it is booked?

Both the Guest and the Host can cancel a date within 5 hours of unlocking the chat option and a full refund will be initiated to the Host’s Go Dating Wallet. You will find the cancel option on the booking dashboard up to 5 hours after the chat option unlocks.

When is adhaar verification done?

Aadhar Verification will be a mandatory step during the confirmed date booking process. Both the Host and Guest will need to complete verification to ensure the legitimacy of their profiles and enhance safety.

What Happens if I cancel the date 5 hours after the chat option unlocks?

In case you do not cancel the date in the given period, 20% of the value of Go Dating Hearts will be deducted as platform fees. In case the Host cancels the date , the Guest retains the 10% of Go Dating Hearts which reflect in their wallet upon accepting the invite, and 70% is refunded to the Host’s Go Dating wallet. In case the Guest cancels the date, then the host receives the full 80% refund in their Go Dating Wallet.

Do I get a refund if my date does not show up?

In this case, the host gets a full refund. You have the option of reviewing and rating your date. Any individual who gets more than 3 consecutive low ratings (1 star), then they are blacklisted from the Go Dating platform. Blacklisted individuals are not allowed to use the platform for the next 45 days.

How do I report a date?

Go Dating provides a rate and review system to understand the dating experience of each user. This ensures that profiles with consecutive low ratings are blacklisted from our platform. For any further assistance, you can reach out to us on info@godatingapp.com

What are ‘hearts’ on Go Dating?

When do the ‘hearts’ reflect in my Go Dating account?

After a Guest accepts the invitation for a date, 10% of the set amount reflects in the guest’s wallet. The chat option unlocks 24 hours prior to the scheduled date. At the time of the actual date, the guest receives a QR code which must be scanned by the Host in order to confirm the meeting. Following this the remaining hearts will reflect in the Guest’s wallet, if the Guest chooses to keep the hearts as a gift. Guests receive a total of 80% of the amount that they decide upon for each date. 20% is deducted as platform fee.
If the Guest chooses to use the hearts to pay for the date, 80% of the amount is transferred to the partner venue directly by Go Dating. 20% is deducted as platform fee.

How can I redeem my in-app currency?

If the Guest chooses to keep the hearts as a gift, they can visit the marketplace and redeem them against a range of products, based on the purchase value and the hearts available in the wallet.

What happens to my Go Dating Hearts if I switch roles from Guest to Host?

In this case, you can use the Go Dating Hearts in your wallet to book a date.

For how long is my in-app currency valid?

Your Go Dating hearts are valid for as long as your account is active on the platform. You can choose to redeem them as you please or accumulate the hearts over time. However, upon deleting your account, your Go Dating hearts become invalid. In this case, make sure you get in touch with the Go Dating team to inform them about account deletion so that the appropriate redeeming process can be initiated.

If I uninstall my application, do I lose the in-app currency?

No, app uninstall does not affect your Go Dating Hearts. However, if the app remains uninstalled for a period of 45 days, your Go Dating Hearts will become invalid.

How to delete my Go Dating account?

Go to your settings and click on Delete My Account. It’s that simple. In case of Guest accounts, we recommend that you write to us on info@godatingapp.com before deleting your account so that you can redeem your Go Dating Hearts.
